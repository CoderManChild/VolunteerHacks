Frequently Asked Questions

What is VolunteerTech Hackathon?

VolunteerTech is a hackathon focused on creating technology solutions for community and volunteering challenges. It's an opportunity for participants to use their skills to make a positive impact.

Who can participate?

Participation is open to students only. Teams can be composed of up to 4 members.

How do I register?

You can register on our Devpost page linked above.

What should I prepare?

Prepare a project that aligns with one of our hackathon tracks. Ensure your project is well-documented and ready to present during the event.
